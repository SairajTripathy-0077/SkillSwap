Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2025

Welcome to Skill Swap ("us", "we", "or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at connect.skillswap@gmail.com.

When you use our application (the "App") and services, you trust us with your personal information. We take your privacy very seriously. In this privacy notice, we seek to explain to you in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our services, or otherwise when you contact us.

The personal information that we collect includes the following:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name (first and last), email address.
  • User Profile Data: Bio, skills you offer, skills you seek, and verification links (LinkedIn, GitHub, Portfolio).
  • User-Generated Content: Chat messages exchanged with other users through our App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our App for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To manage user accounts. We use your information for the purposes of managing your account and keeping it in working order.
  • To enable user-to-user communications. We use your information (public profile data and chat messages) to enable you to find and connect with other users, request swaps, and communicate with "swap buddies."
  • To protect our Services. We may use your information as part of our efforts to keep our App safe and secure (for example, for fraud monitoring and prevention).

3. How Your Information Is Shared

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations.

Public Profile

Your "Public Profile" data is visible to all other registered users of the App. This includes:

  • Your First and Last Name
  • Skills You Offer
  • Skills You Seek
  • Your Verification Links (LinkedIn, GitHub, Portfolio)

Your email address is considered private and is only visible to users with whom you have an "accepted" swap, and only within the user profile modal.

Chat Messages

Chat messages are strictly private between you and the user you are communicating with. We do not monitor these messages, and they are not visible to any other users.

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures (such as Firebase Authentication and Firestore Security Rules) designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

You have the right to permanently delete your account at any time from your "My Profile" page. When you delete your account:

  • Your Auth user is permanently deleted.
  • Your private profile document is permanently deleted.
  • Your public directory document is permanently deleted.
  • All swap requests involving you are set to "removed" or deleted.
  • All chat rooms and chat messages associated with your account are permanently deleted.

This action is irreversible.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You may review, change, or terminate your account at any time.

If you would like to review or change the information in your account (such as your name, bio, or skills), you can do so at any time by logging in and visiting your "My Profile" page.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this privacy policy frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may email us at connect.skillswap@gmail.com.
